The basic idea is that we are using public records to look for patterns in Irving Police Department data. We are not trying to prove one specific theory or focus only on one charge type. For example, we are requesting all arrests by year, not just public-intoxication arrests, because the goal is to see what the data actually shows.

Some of what we are looking for:

- possible misconduct patterns;
- weak supervision or unusual complaint/use-of-force patterns;
- missing fields, delayed productions, or records-handling issues;
- arrest outcomes that do not match officer activity levels;
- and also exemplar officers whose history shows effective policing, restraint, low complaint rates, and solid outcomes.

Most of the work will be in Google Drive. That is where we will keep requests, correspondence, Attorney General letters, productions, spreadsheets, and review notes. If you are comfortable with Drive, Docs comments, and basic Sheets review, that is perfect.
